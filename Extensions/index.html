QLM Extensions

This directory contains extensions and derived results of the Quantum Lattice Model (QLM).

All works in this directory are derived strictly from the canonical QLM foundations and do not introduce new primitive assumptions, degrees of freedom, or free parameters.

Included Extensions

Planck Energy Density Cap and Saturated Core Completion

This extension demonstrates that the Quantum Lattice Model implies a universal Planck energy density cap and a minimal, non-singular saturated-core interior for gravitational collapse while preserving exact Schwarzschild exterior behavior.

The result follows directly from the canonical QLM foundations and serves as a consistency validation of the framework under extreme gravitational conditions.
